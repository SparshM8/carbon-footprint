CHALLENGE OVERVIEW
================================================================================

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE:
Design a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their 
carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

KEY REQUIREMENT: Beyond Simple Tracking
The platform goes beyond a basic calculator dashboard. It focuses on "awareness 
as an experience" — making invisible data feel personal, urgent, and actionable 
to drive behavioral change.

CORE APPROACH:
1. Make data personal & relatable (translate kg CO₂ into real-world equivalents)
2. Create urgency through contextual nudges at decision points
3. Enable gamification via a "living world" ecosystem
4. Build social accountability through team leaderboards
5. Provide AI-powered personalized guidance

================================================================================
ASSUMPTIONS & LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

ASSUMPTIONS MADE:
1. Simplified emission factors (use country/vehicle averages)
2. Self-reported data (not auto-tracked via APIs)
3. India context (5.2 kg avg, grid factors calibrated)
4. Team self-organization (no workplace SSO/LDAP)
5. AI responses for general guidance, not expert advice
6. Single user per device (no multi-user login)
7. English language support only

KNOWN LIMITATIONS:
1. Emission factors are approximations (±15% accuracy typical)
2. No backend persistence (data stored client-side only)
3. No analytics or usage tracking
4. Limited to mobile viewport (no desktop optimization)
5. No export/sharing of historical data
6. AI advisor requires active API key (graceful fallback included)
